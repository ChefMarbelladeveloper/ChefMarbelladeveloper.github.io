Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

TipShare is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect essential information necessary for the proper functioning of the application:

  • Employee names and roles
  • Tip distribution data
  • Contact information for notifications

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store any personal information on external servers.

Notifications

When you enable notifications:

  • Messages are sent directly through SMS/WhatsApp
  • Contact information is used solely for tip distribution notifications
  • No contact information is shared with third parties

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Modify your information
  • Disable notifications
