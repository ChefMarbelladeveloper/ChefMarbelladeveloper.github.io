Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

TipShare is committed to protecting your privacy. We only collect essential information necessary for the application to function.

The information we collect includes employee names and tip amounts, which are used solely for tip calculation and distribution purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on external servers.

Notifications

SMS and WhatsApp messages are sent directly through your device's messaging apps. We do not store or transmit message content.
